# Plasma Physics

## Plasma Properties
- Debye length: Characteristic shielding distance in a plasma
- Plasma frequency: Natural oscillation frequency of electrons
- Gyroradius (Larmor radius): Radius of charged particle orbit in magnetic field
- Collision frequency: Rate of Coulomb collisions between particles

## Magnetohydrodynamics (MHD)
- Ideal MHD: Frozen-in flux, force balance, equilibrium configurations
- Resistive MHD: Reconnection, tearing modes, resistive wall modes
- MHD stability: Kink modes, ballooning modes, interchange instabilities
- Grad-Shafranov equation: Axisymmetric plasma equilibrium

## Transport
- Classical transport: Collisional diffusion across magnetic field lines
- Neoclassical transport: Drift orbit effects in toroidal geometry
- Anomalous transport: Turbulence-driven transport exceeding neoclassical predictions
- Gyrokinetic simulations: Modeling turbulent transport in fusion plasmas

## Plasma Heating Methods
- Ohmic heating: Resistive heating from plasma current
- Neutral beam injection (NBI): Energetic neutral atoms depositing energy
- Ion cyclotron resonance heating (ICRH): RF waves at ion cyclotron frequency
- Electron cyclotron resonance heating (ECRH): Microwave heating at electron cyclotron frequency

## Key Dimensionless Parameters
- Beta (β): Plasma pressure / magnetic pressure
- Normalized beta (βN): Stability metric for tokamaks
- Safety factor (q): Magnetic field line pitch, stability threshold at q > 1
- Greenwald density limit: Maximum density scaling with plasma current
