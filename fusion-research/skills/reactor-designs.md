# Major Fusion Projects & Designs

## Tokamaks
- ITER (France): Q=10 target, 500 MW thermal, first plasma expected ~2035
- JET (UK): Largest currently operating, achieved 59 MJ in 2021
- SPARC (Commonwealth Fusion): Compact, high-field HTS magnets
- EAST, KSTAR, JT-60SA: Superconducting tokamaks

## Stellarators
- Wendelstein 7-X (Germany): Optimized stellarator design
- LHD (Japan): Heliotron configuration

## Private Ventures
- TAE Technologies: Field-reversed configuration, p-B11 target
- Helion: Pulsed FRC, direct energy conversion
- General Fusion: Magnetized target fusion
- Zap Energy: Sheared-flow Z-pinch

## Inertial Confinement
- NIF (USA): Achieved ignition December 2022
- Laser Mégajoule (France)

Always note the confinement type, target fuel cycle, and current development stage when comparing projects.
